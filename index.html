Work with me(?)

I think when people write “work with me” on their website, there are two possible connotations:

  1. A polite, non-needy way of saying “hire me”
  2. An invitation to join ongoing work at their firm

This work with me carries both meanings: you can hire my consultancy to provide you with services and if you’re looking for work, you can check with me. Whichever the case, you can navigate to Sisyphus Consulting and click the appropriate button.
