Where and weather..

This hasn’t changed since my last update. Right now I’m residing in Ahmedabad. March-June months are typically quite hot here. And they’re getting hotter (until early 2000s the media and “experts” were saying it is due to some Ozone layer hole. Then they started saying that it is due to Global Warming. Now I’m convinced it is due to Climate Change). Let’s see how this season goes.

Watching, reading…

If I’m watching any movies, they’re mostly South Korean, Japanese or Indonesian ones. (This reminds me of compiling the list of movies that I have watched until now.) Currently reading “Barbarians at the Gate: The Fall of RJR Nabisco”, a real corporate drama thriller. I haven’t read something like this ever. The authors are wordsmiths and real artists, painting a vivid picture of each character involved in the LBO (leveraged buyout) of RJR Nabisco. Simply beautiful.
